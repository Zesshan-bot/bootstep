History of Games

In 1952, British professor A.S. Douglas created a game called OXO, now known as tic-tac-toe, as part of his doctoral dissertation. In 1958, the first game for entertainment was created.

What is Gaming

The word gaming originated in the 15th century to refer to gambling in dice or card games. Gaming was used to refer to gambling until the late 1900s, when it was applied to playing recreational board games and pen-and-paper role-playing games.

Types of Games

There are 10 main video game genres, each with multiple subgenres and hybrids. The top 3 game genres are action, adventure, and RPG (role-play games).
