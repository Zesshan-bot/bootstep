GOD of WAR

God of War is an action-adventure game series that follows Kratos, a powerful warrior, as he battles gods, monsters, and mythical beings from different mythologies. The game combines intense combat with an emotional story about family, loss, and redemption. In the latest games, Kratos journeys with his son, Atreus, in a world inspired by Norse mythology.

Call of Duty

Call of Duty: Mobile is a popular mobile game that brings the action of the classic Call of Duty series to phones and tablets. It's a first-person shooter where players engage in intense battles, either in teams or solo, across different modes and maps.

Lords Mobile

Lords Mobile is a mobile strategy game where players build their own kingdoms, train armies, and battle against other players. The goal is to grow your empire by gathering resources, upgrading buildings, and attacking other players or monsters.

Minecraft

Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game where players can explore, build, and survive in a blocky, 3D world. Players can create almost anything by mining resources and crafting tools, from simple houses to complex structures. The game has no set goals, allowing players to be creative and explore at their own pace.

Pubg

PUBG Mobile is a popular online multiplayer battle royale game where players compete to be the last person or team standing. The game starts with up to 100 players parachuting onto a large map, where they search for weapons, supplies, and gear to survive while battling other players. As the match goes on, a shrinking "safe zone" forces players into a smaller area, increasing chances of encounters. PUBG Mobile can be played solo, in pairs, or in teams, and it's known for its realistic graphics, strategic gameplay, and variety of maps.

Raid Shadow Legend

Raid: Shadow Legends is a fantasy-themed, turn-based role-playing game (RPG) where players collect and upgrade heroes to battle through dungeons, bosses, and other players.
